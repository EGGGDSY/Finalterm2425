The Great Gatsby

Looking for Alaska

John Green's heartfelt novel about love, loss, and the search for meaning in life.

The Fault in Our Stars

A moving story by John Green about two teenagers facing cancer and finding love in a challenging time.

J.R.R. Tolkien's classic fantasy novel about Bilbo Baggins' adventure in the world of Middle-Earth.

The Chronicles of Narnia

C.S. Lewis' magical series of fantasy novels that follows the adventures of children in the mystical land of Narnia.

The Shining

Stephen King's terrifying tale of isolation and madness in a remote hotel.

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone

J.K. Rowling's beloved fantasy novel about a young wizard's first year at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.
