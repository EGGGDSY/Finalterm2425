About Our Book Club

What is Our Book Club?

Welcome to BOOKIES! We are a passionate group of readers who come together to explore the wonderful world of books. Whether you're an avid reader or just starting to discover the joy of reading, our book club is the perfect place to engage with others and share your thoughts on the books you love.

Every month, we select a book to read and discuss it in-depth. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive space where readers can exchange ideas, make new friends, and discover new genres and authors. From fiction to non-fiction, from classics to contemporary bestsellers, our book club has something for everyone!

Join us today, and let’s read and grow together. Whether you prefer online meetings or in-person gatherings, we have flexible options to accommodate all kinds of readers.

How We Choose Books

Each month, we choose a book to read based on member suggestions and voting. We believe in making the selection process fun and democratic, so everyone gets a say. Whether it's a timeless classic or a contemporary bestseller, our book selection spans a wide range of genres to suit all tastes.

Our members often suggest books they've read in the past or ones that they’re excited to dive into. We also occasionally host themed months where we focus on specific genres such as fantasy, mystery, memoirs, or historical fiction. At the end of each month, we gather to discuss our thoughts on the book, share personal insights, and connect over our favorite parts.

How We Meet

We offer both virtual and in-person meetings to accommodate readers from all walks of life. Our virtual meetings are held over Zoom and include interactive discussions, while our in-person gatherings take place at local coffee shops, libraries, or even parks, depending on the weather.

We want to ensure everyone has the flexibility to join in, regardless of where they are. Our goal is to create a community of book lovers where readers from different regions and backgrounds can come together, share their love for literature, and build lasting friendships.
